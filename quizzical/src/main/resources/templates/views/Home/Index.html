Quizzical

A web app helping teachers test their students and keep track of them online

About

Quizzical is your reliable assistant for tracking the progress of your students. It's simple and easy to use!

Teacher-users have the opportunity to create network of students, place them in different groups and assign quiz events to the groups (exam quiz for example). Last but not least teacher receives students' results right after the quiz is taken.

Student-users also can keep track of their own results and all information about the ended, active or pending events that are assigned to them.

Make Quiz

Creating quiz is now easier than ever with our user-friendly system. You will be able to store all of your quizzes in one place, order them by categories and modify them. Registering as Teacher gives you the opportunity to make groups, where you can add students. Teachers can create events and set starting time, duration and quiz for the event. They also can choose which group to take part in that event. This functionality is perfect for testing the current progress of your students, because you will receive their result as soon as they take the quiz.

Take Quiz

In order to use the system, students must be registered users. The shortcut for taking a quiz can be used if you know the quiz password. If you don't, you can access your active quizzes and events from the menu.
